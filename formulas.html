Formulas

Welcome to Formulas!
Here you'll find all the formulas and references needed for your studies!

  • Limit definition: \(\lim_{x \to a} f(x) = L\)
  • One-sided limits: \(\lim_{x \to a^-} f(x), \lim_{x \to a^+} f(x)\)
  • Continuity at \(x=a\): \(\lim_{x \to a} f(x) = f(a)\)
  • Intermediate Value Theorem (IVT): If \(f\) continuous on \([a,b]\) and \(N\) between \(f(a)\) and \(f(b)\), \(\exists c \in (a,b)\) s.t. \(f(c) = N\)
  • Definition of derivative: \(f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}\)
  • Tangent line: \(y - f(a) = f'(a)(x - a)\)
  • Power Rule: \(\frac{d}{dx}[x^n] = nx^{n-1}\)
  • Constant Rule: \(\frac{d}{dx}[c] = 0\)
  • Product Rule: \((fg)' = f'g + fg'\)
  • Quotient Rule: \(\left(\frac{f}{g}\right)' = \frac{f'g - fg'}{g^2}\)
  • Chain Rule: \(\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{du} \cdot \frac{du}{dx}\)
  • Derivatives of trig functions: \(\frac{d}{dx}[\sin x] = \cos x\), \(\frac{d}{dx}[\cos x] = -\sin x\), \(\frac{d}{dx}[\tan x] = \sec^2 x\), \(\frac{d}{dx}[\sec x] = \sec x\tan x\), \(\frac{d}{dx}[\csc x] = -\csc x\cot x\), \(\frac{d}{dx}[\cot x] = -\csc^2 x\)
  • Related rates: Differentiate implicitly w.r.t time \(t\)
  • Velocity and acceleration: \(v(t) = s'(t)\), \(a(t) = v'(t) = s''(t)\)
  • Linear approximation: \(f(x) \approx f(a) + f'(a)(x - a)\)
  • Optimization: Use derivatives to find maxima/minima
  • Critical points: \(f'(x) = 0\) or undefined
  • Mean Value Theorem (MVT): \(f'(c) = \frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}\)
  • First Derivative Test — determines increasing/decreasing
  • Second Derivative Test — determines concavity and points of inflection
  • Indefinite integral: \(\int f(x)\,dx = F(x) + C\)
  • Definite integral: \(\int_a^b f(x)\,dx = F(b) - F(a)\)
  • Fundamental Theorem of Calculus: \(\frac{d}{dx} \left(\int_a^x f(t) dt \right) = f(x)\)
  • Average value: \(\frac{1}{b-a} \int_a^b f(x) dx\)
  • Separation of variables: \(\frac{dy}{dx} = g(x)h(y) \Rightarrow \frac{1}{h(y)} dy = g(x) dx\)
  • Slope fields visualize solutions of \( \frac{dy}{dx} = f(x,y) \)
  • Exponential growth/decay: \(\frac{dy}{dt} = ky \Rightarrow y = Ce^{kt}\)
  • Area between curves: \(\int_a^b [f(x) - g(x)] dx\)
  • Volume (Disk): \(V = \pi \int_a^b [r(x)]^2 dx\)
  • Volume (Washer): \(V = \pi \int_a^b [R(x)^2 - r(x)^2] dx\)
  • Volume (Shell): \(V = 2\pi \int_a^b (radius)(height) dx\)
